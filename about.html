In 5th grade, I knew I wanted to be a journalist. I even started my own newspaper for my 5th grade class; it featured my mad Word Art skills and Microsoft Word design chops circa 2005.

Since then, I’ve graduated to InDesign and more distinguished editorial pursuits. In 2012, I moved from my hometown of Tulsa, Oklahoma, to Columbia, Missouri, to study journalism at the University of Missouri. I’ve graduated from that, too, in December 2015.

Currently, I contribute to KC magazine and Feast magazine. This past summer I worked as an editorial intern at KC magazine and as a contributing writer for Vox magazine. City and regional magazines are my jam, but I’m also partial to trend pieces about food, travel and culture.
