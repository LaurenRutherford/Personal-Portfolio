  • The Maneater Student Newspaper

    Managing editor, May-October 2014

    Opinion/Outreach Editor, January-May 2014

    Assistant Editor, August 2013-January 2014

My quest to revamp The Maneater’s social media presence began after attending the Associated Collegiate Press conference in New Orleans in October 2013. I decided that our publication could do a lot better. Long story short, I invented a planning system and introduced our newsroom to Hootsuite. I most formally took on the social media editor role as Outreach Editor.

In spring 2014, my consistent and systematic posting to our accounts gained us NUMBER new Facebook likes and NUMBER new Twitter followers. I helped to implement social media as a method of storytelling by using Storify and posting embeds.

As I transitioned to Managing Editor, I didn’t feel my system was ready to pass along to others. I honed the system, and in October upon my resignation, created a social media editor position. I trained two staff members to run The Maneater’s accounts, and I’m proud to say it’s a position that the staff maintains today. Just last spring, The Maneater Twitter account passed 10,000 followers and earned a verified check mark.
